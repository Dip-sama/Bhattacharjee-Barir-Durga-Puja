Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, all donations to Bhattacharjee Bari Durga Puja are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. We will provide an official receipt for your donation.
How will my donation be used?
Your donation will be used for various aspects of the Durga Puja celebration, including idol creation, pandal decoration, cultural programs, prasad preparation, and community services during the festival.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes, we respect your privacy. You can choose to remain anonymous by selecting the "Anonymous Donation" option in the donation form.
Can I donate items instead of money?
Yes, we welcome in-kind donations such as food items for prasad, decorative items, or other materials needed for the puja. Please contact our committee at contact@bhattacharjeebaridurgapuja.org to discuss your contribution.
How can I volunteer instead of donating?
We greatly appreciate volunteer support! Please fill out the volunteer form on our "Get Involved" page or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@bhattacharjeebaridurgapuja.org.
