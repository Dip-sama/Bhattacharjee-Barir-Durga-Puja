Continue Our Legacy

For over seven decades, Bhattacharjee Barir Durga Puja has been a celebration of tradition, culture, and community spirit. Your generous contributions help us maintain the quality and grandeur of this historic puja while supporting various community initiatives.

Your donations directly support:

  • Idol crafting by traditional artisans
  • Elaborate decorations and pandal setup
  • Daily prasad and bhog preparation
  • Cultural programs during the festivities
  • Community outreach and charitable activities

We express our heartfelt gratitude to all our donors who have been supporting us over the years. Your contribution, regardless of the amount, helps us preserve our cultural heritage for future generations.
